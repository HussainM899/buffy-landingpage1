Why BUFFY?

Most startup ideas fail not because they're bad, but because founders don't get honest feedback early enough. Friends and family are too nice. Advisors are too busy. Investors only see polished pitches.

BUFFY changes that. Our AI doesn't care about your feelings. It won't sugarcoat the truth. It will find the holes in your logic, the gaps in your market research, and the flaws in your business model.

Built by the team at Effect3, BUFFY combines years of startup experience with cutting-edge AI to give you the brutal honesty you need to succeed.
